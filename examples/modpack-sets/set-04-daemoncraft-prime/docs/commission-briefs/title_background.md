# Commission Brief: DaemonCraft Title Screen Background

## Overview
1920×1080 pixel-art landscape for the DaemonCraft Java title screen.
Replaces `assets/minecraft/textures/gui/title/background/` panoramas or serves
as a static background behind the title buttons.

## Budget
$100-200 USD

## Deliverables
- 1920×1080 PNG (static background)
- 1024×512 PNG (downscale for lower-resolution displays)
- Panorama cube faces (6× 1024×1024, optional +$50)
- Source file (.ase or .psd)

## Style direction
**Pixel-art Minecraft landscape at dusk.** Atmospheric, slightly mysterious,
but welcoming. Should make players want to click "Multiplayer" and join the
world.

## Scene description
A view from a hilltop overlooking a forest valley:
- **Foreground:** Cyan-tinted oak trees ( referencing Better Leaves )
- **Midground:** A small village with warm lights, a river reflecting the sky
- **Background:** Purple-pink sunset sky with pixel-art clouds
- **Atmosphere:** Fireflies or particles (subtle, not distracting)
- **No text** — the Minecraft logo and buttons will be overlaid

## Color palette
- Sky gradient: `#1a1a2e` (deep navy top) → `#9b5de5` (purple mid) → `#f15bb5` (pink horizon)
- Trees: `#2ec4b6` (cyan leaves) + `#16213e` (dark trunks)
- Water: `#4cc9f0` reflecting the sky
- Lights: `#fee440` (warm yellow windows)

## Reference concepts
See `docs/ai-concepts/title_background_concepts.png` for 3 AI-generated
panoramas. Preferred: concept #1 (dusk valley with cyan forest and purple sky).

## Constraints
- Must work with Minecraft's title overlay (buttons in center, logo at top)
  — keep the center area relatively calm/low-contrast
- No characters or mobs in the scene (avoids dating the art to a specific MC version)
- Pixel-art style, not photorealistic — align to apparent 32×32 or 64×64 "pixels"
- Loopable horizontally if possible (for panorama conversion)

## Timeline

- Revisions: 5-7 days
- Final delivery: 21 days total
